At Ralph St Laundry, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the data you provide when using our services or visiting our website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, including your name, address, phone number, email address, and payment details when you use our services, create an account, or contact us.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information on how you interact with our website, such as IP address, browser type, access times, and pages visited. This helps us improve our website and services.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: We use your personal information to provide our laundry services, including processing orders, pickup and delivery, billing, and customer support.
  • Communication: We may use your contact information to send updates regarding our services, promotions, or respond to your inquiries.
  • Improvement of Services: We use usage data to understand user behavior and improve the quality and efficiency of our services.

3. Sharing Your Information

  • Service Providers: We may share your personal information with trusted service providers who assist us in delivering our services, such as payment processing and delivery partners. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information. We use secure servers, encryption, and regularly review our security practices to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse of your data.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us remember your preferences and track site usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of our website.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [email address].

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. Any updates will be posted on our website, and you are encouraged to review it periodically.
